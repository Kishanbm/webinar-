The questions members ask most. If your answer isn’t here, use the form above and we’ll get back to you within 24 hours.

How quickly will I get a response after submitting the form?
Our team typically responds within 6 hours during business days, and always within 24 hours Monday through Friday. For urgent technical issues with an active membership, mention “urgent” in your subject line and we’ll prioritize it.
What’s included in the Free Membership?
Five complete strategy courses, bonus resources, and access to our public trade-result archives going back to 2017. No credit card required — start here.
Do you offer refunds on premium memberships?
Yes — every premium membership comes with a 7-day money-back guarantee. If it’s not the right fit, email support@navigationtrading.com within 7 days of your purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.
Can I switch between membership plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from your Whop dashboard. Prorated credit applies on upgrades, and there are no cancellation fees on any plan.
Is the Live Trading Room beginner-friendly?
Yes. We narrate every trade in real time and explain the reasoning behind it. New members tell us this is the fastest way to learn — you see the setup, the entry, the management, and the exit, live. We recommend completing the five free strategy courses first.

We work with a small number of carefully selected partners. If you run a trading-related newsletter, podcast, or community, use the form above with “Partnership Inquiry” selected and our partnerships lead will be in touch.
