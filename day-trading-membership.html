  • You want to learn day trading through live, real-time experience — not just videos. You understand that watching pre-recorded content only gets you so far. You want to see a professional trader make decisions as the market moves and learn from the process, not just the outcome.

  • You want income strategies, trend trading, and portfolio-level approaches. The Day Trading membership focuses specifically on day trading and 0 DTE strategies.
