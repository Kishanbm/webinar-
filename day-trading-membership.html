I'm a complete beginner. Should I start here?
+
If you've never traded options before, we recommend starting with the Free Membership first. It covers the fundamentals — what options are, how they work, and basic strategies. Once you're comfortable with those concepts, the Day Trading membership is where you take it live.
What time are the live streams?
+
The morning session starts around 9:15 AM Eastern, before market open at 9:30 AM. The Power Hour session begins around 3:00 PM Eastern for the final hour of trading. Both run every market day, Monday through Friday (excluding market holidays).
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Your membership renews monthly and you can cancel anytime. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your current billing period.
Do I need a large trading account?
+
No. Many of the day trading strategies — especially the 0 DTE setups — can be executed with accounts of $2,000–$5,000, depending on your broker. The courses cover how to match strategies and position sizes to your specific account. You don't need a big account to start. You need the right strategies for the account you have.
What's the difference between Day Trading and Pro membership?
+
Day Trading ($129/month) focuses on day trading and 0 DTE strategies — 10 courses, daily live streams, and day trading alerts and indicators. Pro ($179/month) includes everything in Day Trading plus 7 additional advanced strategy courses (income spreads, trend trading, portfolio strategies), trade alerts for every strategy, and the full suite of custom chart indicators and watch lists. If you want to go beyond day trading, Pro gives you the complete system.
