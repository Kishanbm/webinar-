Is Day Trading Membership Right for You?

This Isn't for Everyone. Here's How to Know.

Day Trading Is for You If:

  • You want to learn through live, real-time experience — not just videos. You understand that watching pre-recorded content only gets you so far. You want to see a professional trader make decisions as the market moves and learn from the process, not just the outcome.
  • You have at least 1–2 hours during market hours (9:30 AM – 4:00 PM ET). The live streams are where the core value lives. You don't need to watch every minute, but you need some time during market hours. The morning session and Power Hour are the two key windows.
  • You already understand the basics of options trading. You know what calls, puts, and basic strategies are. You don't need to be an expert — but you're past the "what is an option?" stage. If not, start with the Free Membership first.
  • You're ready to commit to a daily practice. Day trading is a skill you build through daily repetition. This membership is designed for people who are willing to show up consistently.

Day Trading Is NOT for You If:

  • You're a complete beginner who hasn't learned the fundamentals. Start with the Free Membership. The 5 beginner courses will give you the foundation you need. Jumping into a live trading room without understanding the basics will overwhelm you, not help you.
  • You want someone else to make all your trading decisions. We share live trades and alerts, but you are responsible for your own trading decisions. This is education and guided experience — not a copy-trade service.
  • You want income strategies, trend trading, and portfolio-level approaches. Day Trading focuses on day trading and 0 DTE strategies. If you want the complete library — income spreads, trend trading, butterflies, calendars — the Pro Membership ($179/month) includes everything.
